Quick logistics

Time zoneVictor and Jackson are both Mountain Time — 1 hr ahead of SF. No change crossing the pass; helps for the early Sunday flight.
Teton PassYour base is ~35–45 min over Teton Pass (WY-22, 8,431 ft) from Jackson & the airport. Rebuilt after the 2024 slide and open, but expect summer construction / reduced speed — check WYDOT road status before airport runs and late-night drives.
Rental carEssential — you'll cross the pass daily; no rideshare at scale here. Reserve now; July sells out.
Park entryGrand Teton is $35/vehicle (7-day), or an America the Beautiful pass ($80/vehicle). (No Yellowstone this trip.)
Bear sprayCarry it in the park — buy or rent in town (can't fly with it). 100 yd from bears, 25 yd from bison/elk.
AltitudeJackson sits at ~6,200 ft. Hydrate; ease into day one.

Classic Jackson Hole — could still add

🔥 = the ones worth squeezing in if there's appetite.

  • Grand Targhee Resort — the Idaho-side ski hill, ~40 min from Victor with no pass: scenic lift rides, wildflowers, easy hikes with huge Teton views. A mellow alternative to the Jackson tram.
  • 🔥 Whitewater rafting — Snake River Canyon — Dave Hansen / Mad River: class II–III splashy fun ~30 min south. Swap in for the calm float if the group wants a thrill.
  • Granite Hot Springs — the scenic alternative to the Astoria soak in the plan: prettier canyon setting, but ~1 hr + 10 mi dirt road. Swap in if you'd rather have the views than the convenience.
  • Dawn wildlife drive — Oxbow Bend / Moose-Wilson Rd for moose, bison and bears (only if anyone's willing to wake early — optional given the vibe).
  • Horseback ride + Western cookout — Linn Canyon Ranch (Victor, no pass) or Mill Iron Ranch: a gentle trail ride + steak dinner. Wholesome and very Western.
  • Dawn wildlife safari — a guided sunrise tour (EcoTour Adventures) to spot moose, bison, elk and maybe bears/wolves in the wild — respectful, from a distance, with spotting scopes.
  • Walk Jackson Town Square — the four elk-antler arches (the classic photo), plus galleries, shops and ice cream; easy to fold into a Jackson evening.
  • Cowboy Coaster at Snow King — an alpine mountain coaster right in Jackson town (~10-min ride, open till ~9 PM mid-summer). Quick thrill, walkable from the Square.
  • Via Ferrata at Teton Village — guided cabled cliff-climbing on Rendezvous Mountain (half or full day, age 12+); big adrenaline with tram views, new Corbet's Couloir routes in 2026.
  • Cascade Canyon hike — carry on past Hidden Falls / Inspiration Point into the canyon: waterfalls, wildflowers, near-guaranteed moose; turn back whenever.
  • Gear up — cowboy hats — Jackson Hole Hat Company on the Square (off-the-rack from ~$45, custom felt from ~$325) before the Cowboy Bar night.
  • Easy Teton hikes — Taggart Lake or Phelps Lake.
  • Clay / sporting shooting — a classic bachelor add-on; a few outfitters run half-day sessions near town.
  • Yellowstone — skipped this trip (too far for a day). Noted here so it doesn't get re-litigated. 😉

📸 Golden-hour & photo spots

Where everyone shoots the Tetons — soft light in the hour after sunrise or before sunset (~8:45 PM in late July).

  • Schwabacher Landing — the postcard reflection of the range in the beaver ponds; best at sunrise.
  • Mormon Row / T.A. Moulton barn — Tetons behind the old barns + bison on Antelope Flats; classic for portraits.
  • Oxbow Bend — Mount Moran mirrored in the Snake River at dawn.
  • Snake River Overlook — the famous Ansel Adams shot; sunset light on the peaks.
  • Signal Mountain summit — sunset over the whole valley (already on Friday).

Other night ideas — mix it up

  • Golden-hour in Grand Teton — stay out for sunset on a park evening: Oxbow Bend or Mormon Row for moose/elk and alpenglow on the range.
  • Stargazing from Victor — Teton Valley has big dark skies; grab blankets and look up from the house — no driving.
  • Victor night, no pass — Knotty Pine live music + Grand Teton Brewing, right in town — skips the late pass drive.
  • Hot tub + fire pit at the house — a low-key night in (Teton Springs has pools/hot tubs on site).
  • A different Jackson bar — the Silver Dollar Bar (live music) or The Rose (cocktail lounge) for a change from the Cowboy Bar.
