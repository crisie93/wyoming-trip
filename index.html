Quick logistics

Time zoneJackson is Mountain Time — 1 hr ahead of SF. Works in your favor for the early Sunday flight.
Rental carEssential — no rideshare at scale here. Reserve now; July sells out.
Park entryGrand Teton is $35/vehicle (7-day), or an America the Beautiful pass ($80/vehicle). (No Yellowstone this trip.)
Sat nightTwo tracks: rodeo group (rodeo 7 PM at the fairgrounds → Fair DJ party) and non-rodeo group (town dinner → Silver Dollar dancing), reconverge at the Cowboy Bar ~10 PM. Carnival + live music run most Fair nights (Jul 18–26).
Book aheadSnake River float (Sat), any group dinner, rodeo tickets. Aerial tram waffles are walk-up.
Bear sprayCarry it in the park — buy or rent in town (can't fly with it). 100 yd from bears, 25 yd from bison/elk.
AltitudeJackson sits at ~6,200 ft. Hydrate; ease into day one.

Classic Jackson Hole — could still add

🔥 = the ones worth squeezing in if there's appetite.

  • 🔥 Whitewater rafting — Snake River Canyon — Dave Hansen / Mad River: class II–III splashy fun ~30 min south. Swap in for the calm float if the group wants a thrill.
  • Granite Hot Springs — the scenic alternative to the Astoria soak in the plan: prettier canyon setting, but ~1 hr + 10 mi dirt road. Swap in if you'd rather have the views than the convenience.
  • Dawn wildlife drive — Oxbow Bend / Moose-Wilson Rd for moose, bison and bears (only if anyone's willing to wake early — optional given the vibe).
  • Horseback ride + Western cookout — Mill Iron Ranch: trail ride + steak dinner. Great group night alternative to the Fair.
  • Easy Teton hikes — Taggart Lake or Phelps Lake.
  • Clay / sporting shooting — a classic bachelor add-on; a few outfitters run half-day sessions near town.
  • Yellowstone — skipped this trip (too far for a day). Noted here so it doesn't get re-litigated. 😉

Eats & drinks

☕ Coffee & breakfast
  • Persephone Bakery — pastries + egg sandwiches; the town go-to.
  • Cowboy Coffee Co. — solid espresso just off the Square.
  • Café Genevieve — brunch in a log cabin; order the "pig candy."
🍽️ Group dinners
  • Snake River Grill — the upscale special-occasion pick.
  • Glorietta / Local — Italian and steak, both great for a group.
  • Bin22 — wine bar + Spanish tapas.
  • Merry Piglets — margaritas + Tex-Mex, loud and fun.
🍔 Local & cheap
  • Hand Fire / Pinky G's — wood-fired + late-night slices.
  • Big Hole BBQ — brisket and ribs to feed a group.
  • Dornan's — pizza + beer deck in the park (Friday lunch).
🍺 Bars & nightlife
  • Million Dollar Cowboy Bar — saddle stools + mechanical bull.
  • Silver Dollar Bar — 2,032 silver dollars in the bar top; live music.
  • Mangy Moose — legendary après bar in Teton Village (pair with the tram).
  • Melvin Brewing / Thai Me Up — award-winning IPAs + Thai food.
🌾 Gluten-free friendly
  • Jackson Drug — dedicated GF fryer, most of the menu GF; the safest bet.
  • Local · Liberty Burger · Thai Me Up — plenty of GF options.
  • GF pizza at Hand Fire or Pinky G's.
